# LinuxCNC AXIS Backplot

To rotate the preview:

1. Open a G-code file in AXIS.
2. Move the mouse over the preview/backplot area.
3. Hold the left mouse button and drag to rotate.
4. Use the mouse wheel to zoom.
5. Hold the middle mouse button and drag to pan.

For the star example, rotate the view to confirm the raised centre and sloping facets are visible.
